About Our Thrilling Adventures

Experience a world of adventures, at our hotel. Immerse yourself in the thrill of water sports, discover coves and appreciate the breathtaking beauty of the sea. Unforgettable moments await you on the shores of east of the island. Create memories by the ocean with us.

Scuba Diving

Explore the breathtaking underwater world at beachside hotel. Whether your a Experienced diver or a beginner, our professional instructors keeps you guided for a safe and memorable journey.

Beach Vollyball

Come and feel the excitement of beach volleyball with your friends or family at our hotel's beach volleyball setup.


Boat Tours

Immense yourself into the blue waters of Sri Lanka. Our well trained experienced guides will lead you to the correct point at the perfect time to whale and dolphin watching at your comfort and safety

Beachside Photography

Capture the pefect moments of sunsets, turtle, birds and other breathtaking sceneries at the shoreline. This will be a perfect opportunity for photographs.
 

Jet Skiing

Hop on a jet ski and feel the adrenaline-rush while exploring the freedom of the open waters.
